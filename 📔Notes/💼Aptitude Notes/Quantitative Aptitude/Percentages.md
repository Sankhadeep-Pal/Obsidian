# Percentages

## 1. Basic percentages

$$
\begin{align*}
1 &= 100\% & \quad \frac{1}{2} &= 50\% \\ \\
\frac{1}{3} &= 33.33\% & \quad \frac{1}{4} &= 25\% \\ \\
\frac{1}{5} &= 20\% & \quad \frac{1}{6} &= \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{1}{2} = 16.66\% \\ \\
\frac{1}{7} &= 14.28\% & \quad \frac{1}{8} &= \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} = 12.5\% \\ \\
\frac{1}{9} &= \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{1}{3} = 11.11\% & \quad \frac{1}{10} &= 10\% \\
\end{align*}
$$

## 2. Change in percentage

$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Change} &= \frac{\text{Final value} - \text{Initial value}}{\text{Initial value}} \times 100\%
\end{align*}
$$

**Trick:** Always try to take **initial value** or **final value** to 100.
